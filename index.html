//entrepreneur

I founded Great Job in 2013. We are bootstrapped, we work relentlessly, and we are hungry for success. Great Job is a design firm that seeks out difficult problems that are worth solving. Nothing is off limits when discovering solutions; we are technology agnostic and not afraid of radical ideas. We believe that simple solutions are not only best, but also the most elegant. Our projects range from apps to aquaponic gardens.

//designer

I am a self-taught designer. Most of my experience comes from working with various startups in the Chicago technology scene. I focus on design strategy and product management, but I have a passion for prototyping and research. I'm not afraid to get my hands dirty in any stage of the design process, from research to testing to mockups to graphics. I am an adjunct product and design intructor at the Startup Institute.
